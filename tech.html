System Architecture

Deep dive into the open-source privacy engine.

01. The Shell (Hardware)

Keepr is built on energy-efficient ARM Architecture, ensuring silent operation and low power consumption (5W idle). It features an integrated UPS (Battery Backup), allowing the system to gracefully spin down during power outages or remain accessible during brief interruptions. No noise. No heat. Just uptime.

02. The Engine (Software Stack)

We believe in pure Open Source software. No proprietary black boxes. Keepr pre-configures the industry's best privacy-focused tools:

  • Immich (Photos): A high-performance self-hosted photo manager. Seamlessly migrate from Google Photos/iCloud. Features machine-learning powered search (running locally on the NPU) and background backup.
  • Nextcloud (Files): The gold standard for self-hosted productivity. Calendar, Contacts, and Files sync across all your devices.
  • Jellyfin (Media): Hardware-transcoded 4K streaming for your movie and music collection.

03. The Safety Net (Redundancy)

Local storage is fast, but redundancy is critical. For heavy users afraid of drive failure, we offer Parity Sync. This optional feature encrypts and shards your critical data to a cloud provider of your choice (S3, Backblaze, etc.) ensuring that even if your house burns down, your data survives. You hold the keys.

04. The Horizon (Roadmap)

Keepr is an evolving platform. Upcoming modules include:

  • Git Server: Host your repositories locally. Avoid big-tech AI scraping your code.
  • Web Hosting: One-click deployment for personal blogs and portfolios.
  • Selective Parity: Choose exactly which folders get cloud redundancy.
  • And many more in the future...
