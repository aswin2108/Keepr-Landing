System Architecture

Deep dive into the open-source privacy engine.

01. The Shell (Hardware)

Keepr is built on energy-efficient ARM Architecture, ensuring silent operation and low power consumption (5W idle). It features an integrated Battery Backup, protecting the device from electricity outages and keeping the server running interruption-free.

Storage is fully modular with a User-Swappable Hard Drive bay, allowing you to easily upgrade or replace drives yourself without tools.

02. The Engine (Software Stack)

We believe in pure Open Source software. No proprietary black boxes. Keepr runs on a custom Open Source Linux kernel, providing total transparency.

  • Immich (Photos): A high-performance self-hosted photo manager. Seamlessly migrate from Google Photos/iCloud. Features machine-learning powered search (running locally on the NPU).
  • Nextcloud (Files): The gold standard for self-hosted productivity. Calendar, Contacts, and Files sync across all your devices.
  • Jellyfin (Media): Hardware-transcoded 4K streaming for your movie and music collection.
  • Git Server: Host your repositories. Avoid AI scraping your code.
  • Web Hosting: One-click deployment for personal blogs and portfolios.
  • And many more modules available out of the box...

03. The Safety Net (Redundancy)

Data integrity is paramount. Keepr supports RAID 0 functionality for performance and storage maximization.

For critical data, we offer optional Parity Sync ("Selective Parity"). This encrypts and shards your elected folders to a cloud provider of your choice (S3, Backblaze) ensuring that even in catastrophic failure, your core data survives.
